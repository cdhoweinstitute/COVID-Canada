During the COVID-19 crisis, the C.D. Howe Institute will update, evolve and publish this “dashboard” on a daily frequency in order to provide a data-driven perspective on the state of COVID-19 cases in Canada and indicators of near-term economic impact.

This will be an evolving product and we will be grateful for feedback on the content. In particular, we welcome comments to identify additional data that may provide insight as this crisis continues.
